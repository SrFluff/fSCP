Agnus Immolatus

Background

WARNING: THIS PROTOCOL IS ONE OF FEW THAT GO DIRECTLY AGAINST THE ORGANIZATION'S MISSION, AND IS SOLEY TO BE USED A FINAL-RESORT. DUE TO THIS PROTOCOL'S AGE IT CANNOT BE STRUCK FROM ORGANIZATION SERVERS.

Agnus Immolatus(Sacrificial Lamb) was first establish near the birth of the organization. Due to it's age and contents, the majority of related information was never, or poorly, recorded. It is assumed to have been created by head council member C-[DATA EXPUNGED].

Core concepts

Agnus immolatus is to be used against spiritual and/or religious anomalies. Such cases would be rapture-adjacent events, mass extinctions of higher and/or divine origin, or other religious end-of-the-word events.

To execute this protocol the organization must keep at least five orphans within deep, live-organism, storage and be fully commited to the execution of all reserved orphans. If the organization deems this protocol necessary then they must all be layed down on an altar of any type and stabbed through the heart, one by one. Due to factors outside of the organization's knowledge this has been theorized to appease higher beings.

This protocol also demands that the organization personnel tasked with the execution of the designated orphans MUST be of a religion practiced by over 20% of the modern world. All sacrifices and altars must be held outside and below clear skies. The organization reccommends the immediate termination and death of the person who was tasked with the execution.
