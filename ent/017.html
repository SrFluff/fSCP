A-017

Class: Esoteric
Risk: Magnificus

Description

A-017 is a, purely theoretical, god-adjacent entity. Codenamed "Alphaeus" 017 could potentially appear near Earth, posing an extra-planar threat. 017 is theorized to have a form emulating a large ball of light. Some speculations indicate that 017 could be cognitohazardous, and upon viewing it people would be compelled to kill themselves, or begin floating up into the sky.

017 was first proposed on [DATA EXPUNGED], 1999 by Dr. [DATA EXPUNGED] as part of his 'old gods' proposal. 017 is the most legitimate of the four gods proposed, with the most amount of feasible characteristics, and likeliest scenario. The other three gods are not too dissimilar to 017 though. All three other gods, named in descending feasibility:

  • Zebedee
  • Thaddaeus
  • Iscariot
share the characteristics of not having a corporeal form, instead being mostly composed of energy and/or light. Iscariot is proposed to be formed of 'one million hands' and is to theoretically arrive by digging up from the Earth's core, making him a deviation. All other gods are similarly cognitohazardous and capable of eliminating the human race with little perceived effort.

The three other gods would similarly cause a rapture-like event as well. While it is unclear what would happen to people, beyond simply floating up or killing themselves, some theories state that people would 'become trapped within their own mind for millions of lifetimes', resulting in an effective mass torture/anti-rapture. The only protocol currently available to the organization in a situation like this would be the [DATA EXPUNGED] protocol, but to enact this protocol, the organization would require full council support.

Containment protocol

Currently the only theorized method of eliminating 017 and the other proposed old gods would be through a mass sacrifice of the human race. Due to it's contradiction to the Global Cryptid Organization's mission these anomalies have been deemed uncontainable.
