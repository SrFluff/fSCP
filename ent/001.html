A-001

Class: Thaumiel
Risk: Significans

Description

In the most standard way, anomaly 001 is the Global Cryptid Organization itself. 001 is not just the physical facilities that contain our anomalies, but also the personnel, protocols, documents, and actions as well. 001 has existed for about [DATA EXPUNGED] years and was founded by the first member of the head council(C-0). 001 does not answer to any world governments and will use any means deemed necessary to achieve their core mission: to maintain the prosperity and protect the ignorant bliss of the human race. 001 will never and has never worked with outside organizations and any requests to do so will be put under heavy investigation before being refused.

Containment protocol

By the nature of 001's class, it is not to be contained by to be used to contain other anomalies. Even if 001 was deemed in such a state where it cannot maintain and uphold it's core mission it is not to be contained but to be completely and utterly eviscerated with no trace of it left behind. 001 poses enough of a threat if gone rogue where containment is to be completely ignored and destruction is to be enacted.

When it comes to containing anomalies the organization owns [DATA EXPUNGED] facilities(as of [DATA EXPUNGED]) where they contain all known and recorded anomalies.
