Scorched Earth Protocol

Background

The scorched Earth protocol was the first, and is the most drastic, of all organization developed protocols and is to be used solely as a last-resort protocol in a situation where all other protocols would fail to preserve the world, the human race, and the organization. This policy may only be enacted by the Global Cryptid Organization's head council, unless exigent circumstances render the head council inept.

Core concepts

The scorched Earth policy will result in the utter and complete destruction of not only the planet Earth but also the reality in which it resides. While this protocol is more nonspecific than other, by sheer nature of its seniority, it is to result in the following WITHOUT EXCEPTION:

  • The complete destruction of the planet Earth(and by extension the human race)
  • The permanent removal of all traces of human impact on the entirety of the reality
  • The complete and utter destruction and removal of any chances or hopes of restoring humanity or, by extent, the organization.

While the protocol may only be enacted by the Global Cryptid Organization's head council in typical circumstances, the organization does recognize that if this protocol is deemed to be necessary, then the situation is unlikely to be typical. In the event where the organization's head council no longer exists, then all remaining organization faculty are to collectively decide whether or not to execute this protocol. If the council no longer exists but has left specific instructions, then all faculty are to follow direct orders from the council.
